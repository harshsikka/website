metarch.ai

Metarch is building Generalist, Proactive Intelligent Systems in support of Human Endeavor.

100 years from now, it is reasonable to imagine that AI systems will interact with the digital and physical worlds in a similar way to how humans do today, allowing for us to collectively do more.

These systems will be massively multimodal, proactive, continually learning, and explainable. These capabilities are necessary to be able to take on complex work with autonomy and at massive scale.

Modern LLMs have demonstrated a massive capability jump, but they struggle to bridge the gap to actually doing useful things in a scaleable way, even with ancillary tooling.

Our mission is to compress this 100 year vision to the next 5 years.

We're starting w/ building a new kind of AI system, an Action Engine, from the ground up to autonomously do any kind of digital work, allowing you (or your team) to focus on what matters.
